## TASK 2 : CREATE CALCILATED FIELDS

1. profit margin

calculates the percent profit made on total sales. Used for determining the profitability of products, categories, and geographical segments.

2. cost

calculates the cost estimate by minus profit from sales (Sales – Profit). useful in finding out how much is incurred to make money.

3. average  order value

provides the average value of sales per transaction (total Sales / total Orders). measures customer spending per transaction.

4. return rate

determines the ratio of orders that are returned as compared to total orders. assists in finding out products/regions/customers with high returns.

5. shipping delay bucket

classifies the shipping time into categories like Fast Delivery (0 to 2 Days), standard delivery (3 to 5 Days), and delayed delivery (Above 6 Days) according to the number of days from order date to shipping date.
