## TASK 10 : EXPLAIN CHART SELECTION


1. sales trend view 

What question the chart answers : 
how are the sales changing over time?

Why the chart type is appropriate :  
line chart is able to represent trends, progress, and seasonality over time.

What field is used for color, size, label, or filter : 

X-axis: order Date (Month)
Y-axis: sales
filters: region, category, customer segment, date

What design principle you applied : 
simple line chart with a good title and consistent formatting was used to show the trends.

What mistake you avoided :
not using a bar chart to represent time series information.

2. regional performance view 

What question the chart answers :
which region has the highest performance in sales and profit?

Why the chart type is appropriate : 
horizontal bar graph can clearly help in comparing the performance of various regions.

What field is used for color, size, label, or filter : 

rows: Region
columns: Sales
color: Profit
filter: Date, Category

What design principle you applied : 
bar sorting and use of color to represent profit.

What mistake you avoided :
did not make use of pie charts which are hard to compare across several regions.

3. category profitability view

What question the chart answers :
which categories of products yield the highest profits?

Why the chart type is appropriate : 
horizontal bar chart allows an easy comparison of profit among different categories.

What field is used for color, size, label, or filter : 
rows: category
columns: profit
color: sub-Category
filters: region

What design principle you applied : 
consistent colors have been used along with sorting of categories based on profit.

What mistake you avoided :
cluttered tree maps have been avoided in order to make exact comparisons easier.

4. customer segment view 

What question the chart answers :
which customer segment is more profitable?

Why the chart type is appropriate : 
bar chart allows for the comparison of different customer segments.

What field is used for color, size, label, or filter : 
rows - customer segment
columns - sales
color - profit Margin
filter - region

What design principle you applied : 
clear labelling was used.

What mistake you avoided :
use of unnecessary effects

5. shipping performance view

What question the chart answers :
which shipping mode has greatest delivery delay?

Why the chart type is appropriate :
bar chart works well to compare the average delivery days among the shipping modes.

What field is used for color, size, label, or filter : 
rows: ship Mode
columns: average Delivery Days
color: shipping Delay Bucket
filters: region

What design principle you applied : 
meaningful use of colors to indicate the delivery speed.

What mistake you avoided :
avoided line charts cause shipping mode are categorical data.

6. discount vs profit view

What question the chart answers :
what is the effect of discount on profit?

Why the chart type is appropriate : 
scatter plot is best suited for revealing the correlation between discount and profit.

What field is used for color, size, label, or filter : 

X-axis: discount
Y-axis: profit
color: category
size: sales
detail: order ID

What design principle you applied : 
color and size were used to reveal different dimensions such as category and sales.

What mistake you avoided : 
bar charts were not used as they could not reveal the relationship between the variables.

7. return analysis view
